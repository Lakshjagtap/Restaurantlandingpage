Welcome to Atithi Restro

At Atithi Restro, we invite you to indulge in a culinary journey that celebrates both tradition and innovation. Nestled in the heart of the city, our restaurant offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere where the rich flavors of classic dishes meet contemporary culinary techniques.
